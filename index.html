Services we provide

Architecture

We offer architectural services comprising of design, detailed construction documents and construction administration. In addition to this, our architects also provide a wide variety of services including feasibility study, architectural programming and project management.

Interior

Worksphere primarily designs commercial and corporate interiors. Interior design performance is determined on individual and organizational standards. We focus on higher productivity, greater creativity, innovation, increased patronage and sales.

Master planning

Our team combines skills of planners, urban designers, landscape architects and placemaking specialists to offer a full complement of services addressing the built environment’s planning and design.

Sustainability design

We offer specialized sustainability services that create enduring value in durable, efficient and healthy environments through reducing cost, optimizing energy and water consumption.
